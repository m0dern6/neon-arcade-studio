Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Welcome to Neon Arcade Studio. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. Specifically, we collect:

  • Google Play Games Data: When you sign in using Google Play Games, we collect your basic profile information (such as your username and player ID) to track your high scores and achievements.
  • Gameplay Data: We store your high scores and game progress via Firebase Cloud Firestore to ensure your data is synced across your devices.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving the game.
  • Authenticating your account via Google / Firebase.
  • Displaying your scores on leaderboards.

3. Third-Party Services

Our game uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your gameplay data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you can do so by contacting us or managing your connected apps within your Google Account settings.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@neonarcade.studio.
