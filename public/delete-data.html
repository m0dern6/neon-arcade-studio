Account and Data Deletion Request

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you would like to request the deletion of your account and all associated data from our game, please follow the instructions below:

How to request data deletion:

Send an email to our support team outlining your request. Please include the following information so we can verify and process your deletion:

  • The email address or Google Play Games display name associated with your account.
  • A clear statement that you wish to delete your account and data.

Email your request to: support@neonarcadestudio.com

Note: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose all game progress, purchases, and associated data. We will process your request within 7-14 business days.
